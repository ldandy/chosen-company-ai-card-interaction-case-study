# Family Conversation Cards

A family-friendly conversation card set for mixed ages, especially teenagers and adults. Each card is designed to be fun, quick to understand, and safe for extended family events.

## Use Rules

- Anyone can pass.
- Answer lightly or deeply.
- Keep teasing kind.
- Do not use someone’s answer against them later.
- No correcting someone’s answer unless it is kind and useful.
- The why matters more than the choice.

## Card Fields

Each card uses this structure:

- Card number
- Card type
- Category
- Front copy
- Back copy
