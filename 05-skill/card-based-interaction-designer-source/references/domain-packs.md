# Domain Packs

Use these as domain lenses. They are not fixed decks.

## Family connection

Goal: Create low-pressure conversation, fun, and mutual understanding across ages.
Best formats: Pick one, would you rather, pick 2 of 5, rank them, power with a catch, build your kit, wildcards.
Avoid: family conflict, money, body image, politics, religion as debate, relationship history, trauma, rankings of family members.
Default disclosure: Level 0 to 3.
Suggested ratio: 60% fun, 25% curious, 10% meaningful, 5% wildcard.

## Team culture

Goal: Help teams discuss norms, support, collaboration, and behaviour safely.
Best formats: scenario, rank them, pick 2 of 5, start-stop-continue, choose your role, support kit.
Avoid: public critique of named people, forced vulnerability, culture blame, manager judgement.
Default disclosure: Level 0 to 2.

## Change management

Goal: Help people understand change, identify support needs, practise decisions, and move toward action.
Best formats: scenario, support kit, rank support needs, decision fork, spot the risk, tiny experiment, explain it to someone new.
Avoid: why are people resisting, what are you afraid of, public frustration capture, forced positivity.
Default disclosure: Level 0 to 2.
See change-management-mode.md.

## AI adoption

Goal: Build confidence, shared language, safe experimentation, and practical use cases.
Best formats: pick first use case, spot the risk, myth or fact, explain it to a colleague, choose the guardrail, tiny experiment.
Avoid: replacing people framing, shame about skill level, surveillance language, hype-only prompts.
Default disclosure: Level 0 to 2.

## Onboarding

Goal: Help new people learn norms, decisions, tools, language, and common situations.
Best formats: scenario, what would you do, choose your role, myth or fact, explain it back, build your kit.
Avoid: insider jokes that exclude, sink-or-swim framing, hidden tests.
Default disclosure: Level 0 to 1.

## Training reinforcement

Goal: Help people remember, practise, and apply learning.
Best formats: myth or fact, scenario, choose next step, spot the risk, teach-back, rank actions.
Avoid: trick questions unless the answer guidance is clear.
Default disclosure: Level 0 to 1.

## Personal growth

Goal: Support reflection, values, small actions, and self-understanding.
Best formats: pick one, values trade-off, tiny experiment, future headline, support kit, private reflection.
Avoid: forced public vulnerability, trauma processing, advice-giving without consent.
Default disclosure: Level 1 to 4 depending on setting.

## Strategy and decision-making

Goal: Help groups compare trade-offs, risks, priorities, assumptions, and next steps.
Best formats: rank them, decision fork, pick 2 of 5, spot the risk, future headline, red flag / green flag.
Avoid: false consensus, unclear decision rights, performative participation.
Default disclosure: Level 0 to 2.
